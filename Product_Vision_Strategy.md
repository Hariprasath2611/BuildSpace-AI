*   **Tagline:** *"The AI Operating System for Modern Construction."*

BuildSpace AI ingests real-time site images, voice notes, BIM models, and schedules to build a dynamic digital twin. It predicts schedule delays, identifies quality defects, and automates daily logs, reducing project delays by 25% and cost overruns by 15% via proactive, voice-first mobile workflows.

## Section 2: Industry Challenges & Problem Statement

Below is an analysis of the core challenges faced by construction teams, detailing their root causes, impacts, and the limitations of legacy tools:

*   **Project Delays & Overruns:** Driven by late detection of scheduling conflicts, material shortages, and weather impacts. Cost overruns average 15–30% of contract value, eroding builder margins.
*   **Manual Progress Tracking:** Field managers spend hours writing daily logs, timesheets, and safety reports. Legacy tools replace paper forms with cloud forms, but still require manual data entry.
*   **WhatsApp & Informal Coordination:** Field crews use WhatsApp to share progress, resulting in unstructured data silos that lack audit trails and lead to design clashes.

## Section 13: Product Principles

*   **AI-First:** AI operates as the core runtime, generating proactive suggestions for every user action.
*   **Field-First:** Mobile workflows are optimized for one-handed use under direct sunlight, minimizing screen time on site.
*   **Offline-First:** SQLite database sync ensures full functionality in low-connectivity areas (e.g., basements).
